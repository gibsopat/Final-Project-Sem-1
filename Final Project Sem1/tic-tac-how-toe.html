Tic-Tac-Toe

The classic, hand-drawn game, brought to life on your screen!

How to Use

This tool has everything you need to play a classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe! Simply choose someone to be Xs, the other Os, and start! The first to get a line of their symbol wins! Feel free to play multiple rounds, such as best two-out-of-three, by refreshing the page after each game and keeping count of how many points (rounds) each player has.

Game Settings

Through the two buttons (one for Xs and one for Os), you and your friend can customize there color, adding some fun and personality to your games!
